# PRESS RELEASE — Acme Corp closes $80M Series C led by Sequoia Capital

**LISBON, Portugal — March 19, 2026** — Acme Corp, the European e-signature
and document workflow company, today announced the completion of an
**$80 million Series C** funding round led by **Sequoia Capital**, with
participation from existing investors Index Ventures, Atomico, and Notion
Capital.

The round brings total funding raised to **$148 million** since the
company's founding in 2014 and values Acme at **approximately $640 million**
post-money.

## Use of proceeds

Acme will deploy the new capital across four priorities:

1. **United States expansion.** Opening of a New York office in Q3 2026,
   with initial hiring focused on enterprise sales and developer relations.
2. **Strategic acquisitions.** Acme has identified four EU-based document
   workflow companies as potential acquisition targets in 2026.
3. **Engineering capacity.** Net headcount additions of 38 engineers in
   2026, with priorities in Rust systems development, SRE for the new
   Madrid office, and developer experience.
4. **Working capital and runway.** Extending operational runway through
   2030 without further dilution.

## About Acme Corp

Acme Corp builds developer-facing APIs for transactional document workflows.
Its flagship product, **Acme Bolt**, processes more than **20 million
signature events per month** for over **1,240 customers** globally,
including 30 of the FT 500.

Acme is headquartered in Lisbon, Portugal, with offices in São Paulo and
(opening June 2026) Madrid. The company is **ISO 27001-certified** and
**SOC 2 Type II-attested**.
