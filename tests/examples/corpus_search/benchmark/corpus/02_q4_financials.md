# Acme Corp — Q4 2025 Financial Report

## Headline numbers

- **Q4 2025 revenue: $42.3 million** (up 24% YoY from $34.1M in Q4 2024)
- **Q4 2025 net income: $7.1 million** (margin 16.8%)
- **Full-year 2025 revenue: $154.2 million** (32% YoY growth)
- **Full-year 2025 net income: $24.6 million**

## Revenue by region (Q4 2025)

| Region | Revenue (USD) | Share |
|--------|---------------|-------|
| **EMEA** | **$18.7M** | 44.2% |
| **Americas** | $14.1M | 33.3% |
| **APAC** | $9.5M | 22.5% |

EMEA was the **best region in terms of sales**, contributing 44% of total
quarterly revenue. The strongest growth came from Latin America, where
revenue grew 51% YoY following the launch of the São Paulo office in Q2 2025.

## Customer metrics

- **Total active customers: 1,240** (Q4 2024: 943, +31%)
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR): 118%**
- **Gross margin: 81.3%**
- **CAC payback: 14 months**

## Subscription mix

- Enterprise (>$100K ARR): 42% of revenue, 8% of customers
- Mid-market: 38% of revenue, 31% of customers
- Self-serve: 20% of revenue, 61% of customers

## Cash position

Cash and equivalents at year-end: **$112M**, including the $80M Series C
proceeds received on March 19, 2026 (booked in Q1 2026 results, not Q4).

## Outlook

Management's guidance for FY2026 revenue is **$200-210M** with operating
margin in the 18-22% range. Key growth bets:
- US expansion (target 30% revenue from Americas by Q4 2026)
- Acme Bolt API v3 launch (Q3 2026)
- Acquisitions (M&A pipeline of 4 targets in EU markets)
