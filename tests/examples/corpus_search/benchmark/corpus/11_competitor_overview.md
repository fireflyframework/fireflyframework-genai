# DocuStream Corp — Company Overview

DocuStream Corp is a B2B SaaS company headquartered in Berlin, Germany. It was
founded on **September 3, 2012** by **Jordan Kim** (CEO) and **Lisa Hoffmann** (CTO).
As of January 2026, DocuStream employs **620 people** across offices in Berlin (HQ),
Amsterdam, and Singapore.

## Leadership

- **Jordan Kim** — Chief Executive Officer (since founding)
- **Lisa Hoffmann** — Chief Technology Officer (since 2016)
- **Oliver Braun** — Chief Financial Officer (since 2020)
- **Ravi Mehta** — Chief Revenue Officer (since 2023)

## Mission

DocuStream builds enterprise-grade APIs for contract lifecycle management and
electronic signatures. The flagship product is **DocuStream Sign**, a document
routing and e-signature platform launched in 2015.

## Headquarters

The corporate headquarters are at **Unter den Linden 40, 10117 Berlin, Germany**.
The Berlin office houses 310 employees.

## Service Level Agreement

DocuStream Sign offers a **99.95% monthly API availability SLA**. Credit schedule
mirrors standard industry tiers: 10% for <99.95% ≥99.90%, 25% for <99.90% ≥99.50%.

## Rate limits (DocuStream Sign API v4)

- `/sign/*`: **500 requests per second** per tenant with 2× burst for 30s
- `/documents/*`: 2,000 RPS per tenant
- `/audit/*`: 150 RPS per tenant

## Security certifications

DocuStream holds **ISO/IEC 27001:2022** certification (last audit: October 2025)
and has completed **SOC 2 Type II** (period ending September 2025). All data at
rest is encrypted with **AES-256**; all data in transit uses **TLS 1.3**.
Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for all admin users.

## Funding

DocuStream completed a **Series D** round of **$120 million** in November 2025,
led by **Index Ventures**. Post-money valuation is approximately **$950 million**.
