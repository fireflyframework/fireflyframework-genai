# Acme Corp — Q1 2026 All-Hands Meeting Minutes

Date: **2026-03-21**, 14:00–16:30 WET. Location: Lisbon HQ + remote.
Attendees: ~440 of 480 employees. Chair: Sam Patel (CEO).

## Agenda

1. Series C announcement
2. Workforce changes
3. Madrid office opening
4. FY2026 OKRs
5. Q&A

## 1. Series C announcement

Sam announced the closing of an **$80 million Series C** funding round on
**March 19, 2026**, led by **Sequoia Capital** with participation from
existing investors (Index Ventures, Atomico, Notion Capital). Post-money
valuation: **~$640M**.

Use of funds:
- US market expansion (estimated ~30% of round)
- Acquisitions (4 EU targets in pipeline)
- Engineering hiring (38 net adds in 2026)
- Working capital and runway extension to 2030

## 2. Workforce changes

The company announced a **5% reduction in workforce** effective March 31,
2026 — approximately **24 positions** affected. Reductions concentrated in:

- Customer support (Tier 1) — replaced by self-serve and AI assist
- Sales operations — consolidating EU regional ops into Lisbon

Severance: 3 months base salary, 6 months extended health coverage,
outplacement services. Sam emphasised that "this is not about the company
struggling — we are growing, but specific functions are being restructured
to scale better."

## 3. Madrid office opening

Acme is opening a new office in **Madrid** at Calle de Alcalá 21, scheduled
to be operational by **June 1, 2026**. Initial capacity 60 desks, focused on:

- Site Reliability Engineering (3 hires planned)
- Spanish-speaking customer success
- Iberia regional sales

Priya Naidoo (COO) noted that the Madrid lease replaces the smaller WeWork
office Acme had been using since 2023 and saves ~€180K annually.

## 4. FY2026 OKRs

Marcus Lee (CFO) walked through the company-level OKRs:

- **O1**: Reach $200M ARR by year-end (currently ~$160M run-rate).
- **O2**: Hit 99.95% API availability SLO (current trailing-90: 99.91%).
- **O3**: Ship Acme Bolt API v3 GA by 2026-09-30.
- **O4**: Maintain >115% Net Revenue Retention.

## 5. Q&A highlights

- **On remote work**: confirmed remote-first policy continues; no return-to-
  office mandate planned.
- **On AI**: Inès Laurent confirmed an internal AI working group has been
  formed, exploring LLM-assisted document parsing for Acme Bolt v4.
- **On the Brazilian market**: revenue from Brazil grew 51% YoY; the team
  is evaluating opening a Rio de Janeiro office in 2027.

Meeting adjourned at 16:32. Recording available on the internal portal
through April 30, 2026.
