# Acme Corp — Company Overview

Acme Corp is a fictional B2B SaaS company headquartered in Lisbon, Portugal. It was
founded on **April 12, 2014** by **Sam Patel** (CEO) and **Inès Laurent** (CTO).
As of January 2026, Acme employs **480 people** across offices in Lisbon (HQ),
Madrid, and São Paulo.

## Leadership

- **Sam Patel** — Chief Executive Officer (since founding)
- **Inès Laurent** — Chief Technology Officer (since founding)
- **Marcus Lee** — Chief Financial Officer (since 2019)
- **Priya Naidoo** — Chief Operating Officer (since 2022)
- **María García** — Data Protection Officer (since 2023)

## Mission

Acme builds developer-facing APIs for transactional document workflows.
The flagship product is **Acme Bolt**, an electronic signature and document
routing platform launched in 2017.

## Headquarters

The corporate headquarters are at **Rua das Janelas Verdes 47, 1200-690 Lisboa,
Portugal**. The Lisbon office houses 280 employees including all C-suite roles.

## Subsidiaries

- Acme Iberia SL (Spain)
- Acme Brasil Soluções Ltda (Brazil)

## Stock structure

Acme is privately held. Following the Series C funding round in March 2026,
the post-money valuation is approximately **$640 million**.

## Industry recognition

Acme was named one of the "Top 50 European SaaS Companies to Watch" by Sifted
in 2024 and 2025. Sam Patel was profiled in the Financial Times in October 2025
under the headline "The Lisbon native quietly building Europe's e-signature
backbone."
