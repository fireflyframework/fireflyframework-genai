# Acme Corp — Employee Handbook (Excerpts)

Last revised: 2026-02-15. Effective for all Acme employees globally.

## Working model

Acme is **remote-first**. There is no requirement to come into a physical
office; employees may choose any location within their employment country.
Lisbon, Madrid, and São Paulo offices are available as optional workspaces,
not as required attendance.

## Vacation and time off

- **Annual paid vacation: 30 working days** per calendar year for all
  full-time employees globally. Pro-rated for partial years.
- **Sick leave**: unlimited, with manager notification required.
- **Parental leave**: 16 weeks fully paid for primary caregiver, 8 weeks
  for secondary caregiver, regardless of country of employment.
- **Sabbatical**: 4 paid weeks after 5 years of service.
- **Public holidays**: per country of employment.

## Compensation philosophy

Acme uses a **transparent salary band** structure. Bands are reviewed
annually using Radford and OpenComp benchmarks. All offers are at the band
midpoint or higher; below-midpoint offers require executive approval.

## Retirement and savings

- **United States employees**: 401(k) plan with **6% company match**, fully
  vested immediately, administered by Fidelity.
- **Portugal employees**: contribution to PPR (Plano Poupança Reforma) up
  to €1,500 / year matched 1:1.
- **Spain employees**: Plan de Pensiones with monthly contribution up to
  €100 / month matched 1:1.
- **Brazil employees**: Previdência Privada with up to 6% gross-salary match.

## Equity

All full-time employees receive **stock options**:
- Joining grants vest over **4 years** with a 1-year cliff.
- Refresh grants vest over **4 years** with no cliff.
- Strike price: 409A fair-market-value at the time of grant.

## Health and wellness

- **Private health insurance** for employees and dependents (premium fully
  paid by Acme).
- **Mental wellness stipend**: €100/month for therapy, mindfulness apps,
  or coaching.
- **Annual home-office stipend**: €1,200 for desk, chair, monitor, etc.
- Quarterly company-paid offsite days for each team.

## Code of conduct

Acme has zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or retaliation.
Reports may be made to HR or anonymously through the third-party hotline
operated by Convercent. Investigations are completed within 30 days.

## Performance reviews

- **Two formal reviews per year** (June and December).
- Review inputs: self-assessment, manager assessment, two peer feedbacks.
- Calibration sessions at the team and department level before final
  ratings are communicated.
- Promotions: any time of year, with a written promotion case approved by
  the skip-level manager.

## Onboarding

New hires complete a **2-week structured onboarding** program, including
shadowing in their function, a paired mentor, and a presentation back to
the team in week 3.
